## 2. Important Product Context

AI Content Risk Score is a software platform that analyzes visual assets for intellectual-property risk, brand-safety signals, and provenance/content-credential information. The service is designed to help teams review AI-generated or AI-assisted media before publication. It is not a law firm, does not provide legal advice, and does not replace human legal, compliance, or editorial review.

Because the Services involve user-uploaded media and AI-assisted analysis, some of the information we process may include images, video, text, and derived findings relating to real people, brands, logos, or other protected subject matter. You are responsible for making sure you have the rights and permissions needed to submit that material to us.

## 3. Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information, depending on how you use the Services.

### A. Information you provide directly

- Contact information, such as your name, email address, company name, job title, and support or sales correspondence.
- Account and workspace information, such as login credentials, user profile data, tenant or workspace membership, team role, and invitation details.
- Billing and transaction information, such as subscription selections, order history, billing status, Stripe customer IDs, payment intent identifiers, and receipt-related details. Payment card data is processed by Stripe and is not stored by us.
- Customer Content, such as images, video, filenames, brand guidelines, restricted vocabulary, policy documents, prompt-like instructions, comments, and other materials you upload or submit.
- Communications, such as emails you send us, support requests, survey responses, and webinar or demo requests.

### B. Information collected automatically

- Device and browser information, such as browser type, operating system, approximate geo-derived region, referral source, and app or browser settings.
- Network and usage information, such as IP address, hashed IP values used for security and rate limiting, page visits, click activity, feature usage, session identifiers, timestamps, error logs, and diagnostic events.
- Cookie, local storage, and similar technology data, such as authentication/session cookies, anonymous scan session IDs, theme preferences, and analytics identifiers.
- Security and audit information, such as login events, tenant-switch actions, purchase and quota events, and administrative activity logs.

### C. Information we generate or derive

- Scan results and risk findings, including IP risk score, safety score, provenance or C2PA status, findings summaries, mitigation recommendations, and related metadata.
- Upload metadata, such as MIME type, file size, checksums, asset IDs, storage paths, retention scheduling fields, and processing timestamps.
- Workspace and entitlement data, such as plan tier, usage limits, scan counts, seat limits, report access, and audit-log availability.

### D. Information from third parties

- Authentication, identity, and session information from Supabase authentication services.
- Billing and transaction status from Stripe.
- Email delivery information from Resend.
- Analytics data from PostHog.
- AI-processing responses and file-processing status from Google Gemini API services.
- Hosting, storage, and infrastructure telemetry from vendors that help us operate the Services.
